Slewing Ring Drives and Bearings
Slew drive also can be called slew drive gearbox. Most of our slewing ring drive is kind of worm gear reducer. And the slew ring bearing is a gear drive part. 
The slewing drive is a full-cycle rotary reduction transmission mechanism integrated with a driving power source. 
  • VE Vertically-Mounted Slewing Drive
  • SE Helical Tooth Slewing Drive
  • WE Curved Gear Tooth Slewing Drive
  • Single-Row Ball Type Slewing Ring
  • Double-Row Ball Type Slewing Ring

Fuyong is one of the China leading producers of slewing ring drives and bearings. With a wide range of dimensions and designs, our worm slew drives and slewing ring are popular around the world. The slew drives and slewing bearing widely used in engineering machinery, coal mining machinery, fire equipment, industrial robots, photovoltaic power trackers, aviation and military defense.
